# 1. Project Vision

Credit Card Intelligence Platform (CCIP) is a Saudi-focused platform that helps users discover, compare, evaluate, and maximize credit card rewards and loyalty programs.

The platform serves as a trusted reference for credit cards, rewards programs, offers, benefits, and recommendation logic.

---

# 2. Primary Goal

Provide the most accurate and transparent credit card intelligence platform in Saudi Arabia.

---

# 3. Target Users

- General consumers
- Credit card users
- Rewards enthusiasts
- Cashback users
- Frequent travelers
- Loyalty program members

---

# 4. Core Value Proposition

Users can:

- Explore credit cards
- Compare cards
- Calculate rewards
- Discover offers
- Receive personalized recommendations

---

# 5. MVP Scope

The first version will include:

### Included

- Credit Card Database
- Loyalty Program Database
- Reward Rules Engine
- Offer Engine
- Reward Calculator
- Recommendation Engine
- Merchant Database
- MCC-Based Classification

### Excluded

- Budget Tracking
- Personal Finance Management
- Open Banking Integrations
- Bank Account Aggregation
- Expense Categorization
- Transaction History Storage

---

# 6. Authentication Strategy

Authentication is optional for MVP.

Guest users can access the platform.

Registered users may receive additional functionality in future releases.

---

# 7. Revenue Model

Phase 1

- Free Platform
- Advertising Revenue

Phase 2

- Premium Subscription (Pro)

---

# 8. Official Data Sources

Only official sources are allowed.

Examples:

- Bank websites
- Official card terms and conditions
- Official loyalty programs
- Official promotional offers

---

# 9. Core Differentiator

The primary differentiator of CCIP is the Recommendation Engine.

The recommendation engine analyzes spending patterns, reward rules, MCC mappings, loyalty programs, and active offers to identify the most suitable credit card for each user.

---

# 10. Future Expansion

Potential future features:

- Premium analytics
- Advanced recommendation models
- User profiles
- Saved cards
- Saved calculations
- Mobile applications
- AI-powered card advisor
